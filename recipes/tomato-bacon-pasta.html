Tomato Bacon Pasta

Description

About as simple a recipe that you can get, this mainly just requires bacon, chopped tomatoes, and pasta! So this recipe is great in a pinch and is quick to make.

Using this recipe as a base, you can add and alter as much as you need for your situation. Bacon can be replaced with any meat really, or even a can of beans to make it vegan! Any extra vegetables can also be added, making this recipe very adaptable indeed.

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • Pasta (2 cups)
  • Oil
  • 150g Bacon
  • Garlic Granules
  • 1/2 Onion
  • Can of Chopped Tomatoes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Parsley

Steps

15 minutes

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan on high heat, and boil the kettle for the pasta
  2. Meanwhile chop the bacon, onion, parsley, and any other vegetables
  3. Pour the boiled water into a saucepan with a pinch of salt. When boiling, put in the pasta and heat according to packet instructions minus 2 minutes
  4. Cook the bacon for 1 minute, then add the onion, garlic, and any other vegetables and cook for 3 minutes
  5. Transfer 1/2 cup of pasta water from the saucepan into the large pan. Bring to a simmer and stir
  6. Add the chopped tomatoes, bring to a simmer, and cook for 2 minutes. Then turn down to medium-high heat
  7. Drain the pasta and transfer it to the large pan. Toss gently for 2 minutes
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  9. Serve with parsley. Feel free to add parmesan cheese too!
