Spicy Risotto

Description

This is a simple dish that is easy to batch cook and it is very filling to boot!

Despite the name this is not a risotto but a kind of beef curry. Anyway risotto sounds more fancy and more importantly, with only 7 ingredients and two vegetables to chop, this is easy to make in a pinch.

Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 1 Onion
  • Garlic Granules
  • Pack of Mushrooms
  • Spice Paste (2-4 tbsp)
  • 1 Beef Stock Cube
  • Rice (1/2 cup per serving)

Steps

25 minutes

  1. Heat up a large pan and put the kettle on for the rice (leave some water for the other pan later). Oil is optional as the mince may have enough juices
  2. Put the required amount of rice and double the amount of boiled water in a covered saucepan on medium-low heat. Check back occasionally as the rice is finished when the water has boiled away
  3. Chop the onion into small chunks
  4. Put the mince in the large pan, stirring frequently and breaking down the mince, and cook until browned
  5. Meanwhile, wash and chop the mushrooms into thin slices
  6. Once the mince has browned, add the onion
  7. When the mushrooms are prepared add them to the pan and heat until they reduce down a bit
  8. Pour a little of the remaining water from the kettle into the large pan along with the beef stock cube, the spice paste, and the garlic granules
  9. Heat until most of the water disappears, then add salt and pepper to taste
  10. Serve the rice on a plate or shallow bowl and stir in a few scoops of the sauce
